# VAST Validation Policy

VAST creatives must be approved and free of blocking runtime errors before scale-up. Rejected creatives, missing media files, missing companion assets, wrapper timeouts, and tracking pixel failures require review before delivery is expanded.

Creative quality findings should identify the affected creative, error code, severity, validation timestamp, and recommended operational owner. A rejected or invalid creative must remain out of service until the corrected asset is validated.

Read-only governance tools may summarize validation history and suggest review steps. They must not mark creatives approved, mutate validation records, or pause campaigns.
