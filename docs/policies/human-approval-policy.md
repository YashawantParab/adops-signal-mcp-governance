# Human Approval Policy

AI-generated recommendations that affect targeting, bid floors, budget allocation, inventory expansion, frequency caps, creative serving, campaign pacing, or flight setup require human approval before execution.

Approval records must include the recommendation, reviewer identity, decision timestamp, decision status, and rationale. Rejections are retained with the same audit quality as approvals.

An MCP governance tool may retrieve facts, policy context, and history for a reviewer. It must remain read-only and must not approve, reject, execute, or simulate a successful campaign mutation.
