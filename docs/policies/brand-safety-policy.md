# Brand Safety Policy

Campaigns must preserve advertiser suitability controls when expanding delivery. Operators should review sensitive content categories, regulated advertiser verticals, rejected creatives, and VAST quality issues before recommending inventory expansion.

Allowed expansion can include adjacent premium CTV or video supply when the new inventory has comparable brand-safety tiers, publisher quality, and content-category controls. Finance, business news, news, gaming, and youth-oriented placements require extra review because claims, adjacency, and audience expectations can create compliance or reputational risk.

An AI agent may inspect targeting, creative status, validation errors, and prior recommendations. It must not approve unsafe inventory, bypass blocked categories, or change campaign settings without human approval.
