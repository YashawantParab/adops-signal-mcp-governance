# Budget Shift Policy

Budget shifts can materially affect delivery commitments, client outcomes, and publisher allocation. Any recommendation to move spend between inventory segments, channels, campaigns, or supply sources requires human approval and a recorded rationale.

Before recommending a budget shift, the operator or AI agent should compare pacing risk, available inventory, bid competitiveness, brand suitability, and expected delivery impact. Recommendations must distinguish observed evidence from proposed action.

Read-only tools may inspect recommendation history and policy context. They must not change budget, bids, campaign priority, or allocation settings.
