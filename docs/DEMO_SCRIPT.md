# Two-Minute Demo Script

## 0:00-0:20: Prioritize Delivery Risk

“CTV campaign underdelivery is a cross-system operating problem. The delivery operations queue ranks the portfolio by pacing risk and makes the next investigation explicit.”

Show Campaign 1048 at 39% pacing, its delivery gap, budget under review, and **Start investigation**.

## 0:20-0:55: Diagnose With Evidence

Open the investigation and run the default delivery question.

“The orchestrator collects bounded evidence from pacing, setup, targeting, inventory, portfolio pressure, creative/VAST, and bid tools. RAG retrieves operating playbooks. A configured structured LLM ranks the evidence; without a provider key, the interface transparently uses its grounded availability fallback.”

Point to confidence, semantic evidence IDs, root causes, model mode, tool trace, and retrieved sources.

## 0:55-1:15: Prepare Communication

Generate the client-safe brief.

“The external explanation communicates delivery impact and the recovery path while omitting raw auction pricing, publisher loss logs, and unreviewed technical traces.”

## 1:15-1:40: Human Decision Control

Open the decision queue, record a rationale, and approve the controlled inventory expansion.

“The product does not silently mutate a live campaign. Every proposed change remains pending until an authorized operator records why it should be approved or rejected.”

## 1:40-2:00: Verify And Measure

Open the governance record, then the business impact model.

“The final record preserves the investigation, evidence tools, confidence, human reviewer, decision rationale, and timestamp. The editable ROI model turns the product case into a pilot hypothesis measured through time to confirmed cause, expert agreement, approved actions, and protected delivery.”
